7 Small Living Room Decor Ideas That Instantly Upgrade Your Space

If your living room feels cramped, these smart decor ideas will help you maximize space and style.

1. Use Floating Shelves

Wall-mounted shelves add storage without taking floor space.

2. Add a Decorative Mirror

Mirrors reflect light and make rooms feel bigger.

3. Choose Minimalist Wall Art

Clean art designs prevent visual clutter.

4. Add Warm Lighting

Soft LED lighting creates a cozy feel.

5. Use Multi-Functional Furniture

Storage tables reduce clutter in small spaces.
