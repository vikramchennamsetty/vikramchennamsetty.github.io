ElevateLivingCo is a boutique media studio dedicated to bridging the gap between aspirational Pinterest aesthetics and attainable Amazon reality. We believe that a high-end lifestyle is a matter of curation, not just cost.

Who We Are

ElevateLivingCo was founded by Vikram Chennamsetty — a home styling enthusiast who got tired of the gap between the interiors he saw on Pinterest and the price tags attached to them. What started as a personal project to find affordable alternatives to high-end decor grew into a resource used by thousands of readers every month.

We focus on luxury-on-budget interiors: patio upgrades, small-space styling, dark academia aesthetics, and curated Amazon finds that look and feel expensive without the designer markup.

How We Choose Products

Every product featured on ElevateLivingCo goes through a research process before we recommend it:

  • Review analysis: We read hundreds of real buyer reviews on Amazon to identify products that consistently deliver on quality, durability, and visual impact.
  • Visual assessment: We evaluate buyer-submitted photos to confirm that products look as good in real homes as they do in studio shots.
  • Price-to-quality ratio: We prioritize items that punch well above their price point — heavy ceramics, real wood finishes, matte metals — not cheap knockoffs.
  • Bundle compatibility: We test how products work together as coordinated setups, not isolated purchases.
